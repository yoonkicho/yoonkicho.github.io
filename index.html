Yoonki Cho


I am a Ph.D student at Scalable Graphics, Vision, and Robotics (SGVR) Lab at KAIST, advised by Prof. Sung-Eui Yoon. Before joining SGVR Lab, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science at Korea University. My research interests are broad fields of computer vision and machine learning. Particularly, I am interested in image retrieval and representation learning with limited data (e.g. unsupervised learning, domain generalization, etc.).
